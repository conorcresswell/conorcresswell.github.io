About

Hey! I'm Conor Cresswell. I'm an Illustrator, Visual/UI Designer, music lover and part time game enthusiast. Born and bred in Buckinghamshire, I'm currently at the University of Winchester studying Digital Media Design. If my eyes aren't glued to a screen, they're normally glued into my sketchbook as all of my work starts on paper. Inspired by illustrators such as Jamie Hewlett, my work is heavily focused on Graphic Design and Illustration. During my time at University however, i've begun to move into UI and Visual Design. Here, you'll find a mixture of projects i've completed whilst being at University and through my own Freelance work

Dancor Acoustics

The brief here was to create an infographic for Dancor acoustics that had to include the information ‘A team of over 450 people’, ‘17,000ml of metsec’ and ‘10,000m2 of ceiling tiles’. It would also need to follow the Dancor acoustics brand such as the colours, font and incorperating the logo into the infographic.

  • Client: Dancor Acoustics
  • Date: July 2015
  • Service: Illustration

Winchester Silverbacks

For this project, there was no set brief as it was something I undertook myself. As part of the local American Football team, we didn’t have a logo. I therefore created one using already existing American Football logos as a reference point. Using the coach of the team for some feedback if needed.

  • Client: None
  • Date: November 2015
  • Service: Graphic Design

Fight 4 Lives Branding

Designing a website that would explain exactly what his charity ‘Fight 4 Lives’ did, the events they run and additional information. The design had to follow a logo and fonts that had already been created previously. Set text and information was given to me and had to be included on the site.

  • Client: Graeme Hampton
  • Date: September/October 2015
  • Service: UX/UI Design

Echelon Birds and Animals

In this brief, the client wanted 82 different illustrations of animals and birds. They would be created in use for a game Echelon would be developing. The target audience of the game is children between the ages of 3 and 6 using a style that would be simple yet detailed enough to distinguish each animal.

  • Client: Echelon Networks
  • Date: October to December 2015
  • Service: Illustration

The Fox and Hounds

Creatuing simple illustrations that would be used to brand and populate the pub’s website. With this project, they had no set brand guidlines besides the colours used within the pub. I took photographs of items and objects within the pub itself to use as references when illustrating.

  • Client: The Fox and Hounds Pub
  • Date: April 2015
  • Service: Illustration

Steve X Cross

The brief here was to create a logo for my client’s DJ business. The client wanted a simple, professional logo, used a serif font and had that font as the basis for the design. The logo was simplified so that it could be used in either black or white. In addition to this, the client’s name ‘Steve Cross’ had to be incorperated also.

  • Client: Steve Cross
  • Date: July 2015
  • Service: Graphic Design
